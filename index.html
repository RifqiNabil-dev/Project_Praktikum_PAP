Web Browsing

Each search and website visit consumes energy from data centers.

Reduce Your Digital Carbon Footprint

Clean Your Inbox

  • Delete old emails
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters
  • Use spam filters

Optimize Streaming

  • Watch in lower quality when possible
  • Download for offline viewing
  • Use Wi-Fi instead of mobile data

Browse Efficiently

  • Bookmark frequent websites
  • Close unused tabs
  • Use data saver mode
